About the Program

Mastering Workplace Integrity and Ethics for Career Advancement is an interactive online course designed to equip young professionals, recent graduates, and mid-career individuals with practical skills to demonstrate integrity and ethical behavior in Nigeria's workplace. Enhance your employability, build your reputation, and prepare for career advancement.

Program Detailed Overview

The Mastering Workplace Integrity and Ethics for Career Advancement program is a 4-hour online workshop designed to equip Nigerian professionals and job seekers with the knowledge and skills to uphold integrity and ethical standards in the workplace.

Program Structure

  • Duration: 2 days, 2 hours per day (4 hours total)
  • Format: Online workshop via Zoom/Google Meet
  • Methodology: Interactive lectures, group discussions, and scenario analyses
  • Trainers: Experts from anti-corruption agencies and CSOs

Key Takeaways

  • Foundations of Integrity and Ethics
  • Strategies for ethical decision-making
  • Building a professional reputation
  • Handling ethical conflicts effectively

Program Features

  • Comprehensive Handout: Receive a PDF with ethical frameworks and Nigerian-specific resources.
  • Verifiable Digital Certificate: Issued with a unique ID and online verification for credibility.
  • Accessibility: Designed for busy professionals, with online delivery ensuring access from anywhere.

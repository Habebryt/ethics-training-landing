About the Program

Mastering Workplace Integrity and Ethics for Career Advancement is a 2-day interactive online course designed to equip young professionals, recent graduates, and mid-career individuals with practical skills to demonstrate integrity and ethical behavior in Nigeria's workplace.

The course helps enhance employability, build workplace reputation, and prepare participants for career advancement in sectors like banking, technology, HR, and NGOs.
